Origin

Before the words came, there was silence.

Till I was the age of 15, speaking in front of people wasn't difficult, it was paralysing. I'd freeze, lose my place and shrink into the back row whenever a room turned its attention toward me.

What changed wasn't a single moment. It was years of small, deliberate repetitions, from starting my own YouTube Channel to joining clubs, taking the mic when I didn't want to, getting it wrong in front of people and trying again the next week.

That gradual climb took me from a quiet kid avoiding eye contact to a Contest Master at a national Toastmasters conference, multiple recognitions in national public speaking championships and debating championships, hosting/compering in events I never thought I would be able to, and someone who now runs training sessions in diverse subjects for everyone I believe who are capable of excelling themselves they never imagined.

I built and is passionate about this work because I know exactly where the fear lives , and exactly what it takes to talk it down.
